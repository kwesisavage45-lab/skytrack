Safety & Terms

Welcome to SkyTrack Pro. This page outlines our operational guidelines and legal terms to ensure a transparent experience for all users.

Simulation Notice: SkyTrack Pro is a dummy platform designed for simulation and demonstration purposes. It does not provide real-time flight telemetry from actual aircraft.

1. Nature of Service

All flight tracking data, including progress bars, "Live" status updates, and map visualizations, are generated based on mathematical algorithms and user-provided schedules. We are not connected to FAA, ADS-B, or any global aviation radar networks.

2. Safety Guidelines

No Navigational Use: Do not use this platform to coordinate real-world travel, airport pickups, or safety-critical decisions.
Verify with Airlines: For actual flight statuses, always refer to official airline apps or airport terminal flight information displays (FIDS).
Simulated Identity Only: The "Elite Tickets" and IDs generated here are for entertainment only. They are not valid for travel and do not grant access to airport secure zones.

3. Terms of Use

A. Liability: SkyTrack Pro and its developers are not responsible for missed flights, travel delays, or confusion caused by the use of this simulated data.

B. Content Sharing: When sharing screenshots of your simulated tickets, we ask that you disclose that the content is a flight simulation to avoid misleading others.

C. Data Handling: We store your flight data locally in your browser. While we do not share this with third parties, please avoid entering highly sensitive personal information.
